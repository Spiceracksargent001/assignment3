Mecha Classes

Mecha come in a variety of types. Each one varies in scale, and have their own unique capabilities.

Mecha Classes

Note: These classes are categorized relative to the average human body.

  • Real Robot: The type of mecha most commonly associated with the term. Has one of the largest varieties of specific classes than most other Mechas.
    General size scale ranges from 10 to 20+ meters in height.

  • Mini-Mecha: Smaller machines that, while less durable overall, are much harder to hit than Real Robot type Mecha.
    General size scale ranges from around 4 to 10 meters in height.

  • Powered Armor: Mecha whose fundamental designs and capabilities are identical to those of body armor, and tend to be even smaller than Mini-Mecha.
    General size scale ranges from no bigger than the average human to around 3 meters in height.
